SoccerObjectLocationsContainer

public protocol SoccerObjectLocationsContainer

Conforming types are capable of knowing the location of object in the soccer field in relation to themselves.

Note that this means that the values returned by the soccerObjectLocations — since they are in relative coordinate — will be in relation to the conforming type.
